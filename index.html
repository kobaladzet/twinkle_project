The Art of Astrology

Horoscopes, in astrology, are stylized charts depicting the celestial positions at a specific time and location, often used to interpret personality and predict future events. They are based on the belief that the positions of the sun, moon, and planets, along with other factors, influence human affairs. Each of the twelve zodiac signs is associated with a particular period of the year and is believed to correspond with certain personality traits.

Your Sun sign is the astrological sign that primarily influenced the Sun at the moment of your birth. As such, it's the sign of the zodiac that most prominently influences you. It explains you. It reveals your most pronounced personality traits. It even defines, to some degree, who you are, how you perceive the world, and where you allow your thoughts to take you.

Your Moon sign is perhaps your clearest indicator as to the workings of your inner world. It describe your emotional life. Your desires. And the deepest parts of your being and everything that makes you feel emotionally secure. Your considerations that take place beneath the surface and aren't apparent, even to you.

In astrology, the ascendant sign (also known as the rising sign) represents how a person presents themselves to the world and the first impression they make on others. It's the zodiac sign that was rising on the eastern horizon at the exact moment of your birth. While your sun sign reveals your core identity, the ascendant sign influences your physical appearance, mannerisms, and how you approach life's challenges.
